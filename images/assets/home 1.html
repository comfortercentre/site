    Men of Valor

    RCCG QUÉBEC

    The vision and purpose of the “Men of Valor” as follows: Bring men to a saving relationship with the Lord Jesus, Equip men to teach and serve others to be world changers for Christ, Grow men in Spiritual maturity to be Christ-like and to make GOD bigger and better to an unbelieving world (John17:4)

    The man of integrity walks securely, but he who takes crooked paths will be found out. — Proverbs 10:9

    A godly man is a servant, not a slave-master. Being a spiritual leader does not mean dominating your family. It means serving your family, and being a godly example to your wife and children
