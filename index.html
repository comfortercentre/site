WELCOME, THERE IS A PLACE FOR YOU HERE.

Welcome to The Redeemed Christian Church of God, The Comforter Centre (TCC). A place where we build by the Holy Spirit through the word of God for a glorious and impactful living. Jesus Christ is our focus and we raise pacesetters for kingdom exploits and the glory of our God. Here at TCC, we emphasize the love and grace of God and the importance of living by His word. Whether you are coming to church for the first time or you have been in church your entire life, our doors are wide open to people from all backgrounds regardless of where they are on their spiritual journey. We welcome you to join and grow with us in love and knowledge of our LORD, Jesus Christ. Together we will impact our world for good, as ambassadors of Jesus Christ.
